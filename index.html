Purpose

The primary challenge this logistics application aims to address is the inefficiency and inconvenience faced by individuals in urban centers when seeking suitable vehicles for relocating or transporting heavy items.
Currently, users must navigate a fragmented system of local listings and contact individual vehicle owners or rental services, which can lead to delays, increased costs, and logistical stress.
The decentralized nature of vehicle rental services exacerbates these issues, making it difficult for users to quickly and reliably find transportation options that meet their needs, especially during peak periods or urgent situations
