Problem Statement:

Individuals in urban centers face challenges in finding suitable vehicles for relocation or transportation of heavy items due to the decentralized nature of vehicle rental services and lack of a centralized platform, leading to delays, increased costs, and logistical stress.

Goals

The project involves developing a dedicated mobile application that addresses the challenges of finding suitable vehicles for hire in urban centers by providing a centralized platform. This app connects users in need of transportation services with a network of verified vehicle owners or rental services, streamlining the process and enhancing the overall user experience.
